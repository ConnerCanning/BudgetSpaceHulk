Space Hulk is a game in which one player controls Space Marine Terminators and tries to navigate through wreckage
while another player controls Tyranid Genestealers and attempts to hunt the Space Marines.
In this simulation Space Marines (Blue) attempt to cleanse the Xenos scum and Genestealers (Red) attempt to swarm them.
Some cunning Genestealers wait in the walls where they can't be seen to overpower the Terminators with surprise attacks.
This creates a pattern in which typically strong Terminators get caught alone and die to intelligent swarms.
